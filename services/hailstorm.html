Hail Storm

Hail forms in strong thunderstorm clouds, particularly those with intense updrafts, high liquid water content, great vertical extent, large water droplets, and where a good portion of the cloud layer is below freezing 0C (32F). The hail is then part of a storm system, a thunderstorm.
