How It Works

All you need is a simple transmitter. Our transition towers above and below ground will do all the work charging the atmosphere with the correct particles. Then a signal can interact with these naturally occurring particles allowing for them to react to your specific needs.

The signal will need to have a dedicated phone or data line to connect your transmitter to our service center. There we will craft the perfect signal for your device to create a broadcast in your area, which will incite the perfect weather for your needs.

For those super-villains with your personal satellites, we can also create a broadcasting signal that you can send out anywhere in the world. You will need to install our client services on your equipment so the signal can be synchronized with our towers.

Keep the sun in mind for your weather needs

Movement of the earth will affect how concentrated the sunlight reaching earth will be. In addition the angle of the sun will change during the year; this will affect the wavelength of light reaching your area, primarily at dawn and sunset. As some plants respond to certain wavelengths of light for biological processes, you may need to install artificial lighting devices to fully recreate a season.
